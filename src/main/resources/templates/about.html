About our project
A web application that uses Spring, Thymeleaf, and MySQL. Clean Plate Donations is the conduit between Restaurants who have food to donate and Non-Profit organizations who need food. Non-Profits will be able to post a listing requirement such as 10 loaves of bread and if a nearby restaurant is able to fulfill they will contact the Non-Profit. Technology utilized: Java EE, Spring Boot, Hibernate, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
